Tournament Overview

The ITH Football Tournament is an annual intra-departmental competition that brings together students from all levels of the Information Technology and Health Informatics program. This exciting event promotes unity, healthy competition, and sportsmanship among future healthcare technology leaders.

Competitive Spirit

Fierce but friendly competition between academic levels

Community Building

Strengthening bonds between students across different levels

Health & Wellness

Promoting physical activity and mental well-being

Tournament Action

Tournament Format

1

Group Stage

Round-robin format where all 4 teams play each other once. Each match is crucial for securing a spot in the next round.

2

League Points

Teams earn 3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw, and 0 points for a loss. Goal difference serves as tiebreaker.

3

Final Showdown

The top two teams from the group stage face off in an epic final match to determine the championship winner.

Tournament Rules

Match Duration

  • Each match consists of two 45-minute halves
  • 15-minute halftime break
  • Added time at referee's discretion

Team Composition

  • 11 players per team on the field
  • Maximum of 7 substitutes
  • 4 substitution opportunities per match

Conduct & Fair Play

  • Respect for opponents, officials, and organizers
  • Zero tolerance for violent conduct
  • Sportsmanship above all else

Points System

  • Win: 3 points
  • Draw: 1 point
  • Loss: 0 points
  • Tiebreakers: Goal difference, Goals scored

Our Mission

To foster unity, promote healthy competition, and develop leadership skills among ITH students through the beautiful game of football, while maintaining the highest standards of sportsmanship and academic excellence.

Our Vision

To establish the most anticipated annual sporting event in the department that bridges academic levels, creates lasting memories, and builds a strong community of future healthcare technology professionals.
