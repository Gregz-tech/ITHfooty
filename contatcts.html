Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can my team register for the tournament?

Team registration is done through your department's sports representative. Contact your level coordinator or send us a message through the contact form above.

What are the tournament rules and regulations?

Complete tournament rules are available in the official tournament handbook. You can request a digital copy by emailing info@fuhsiithisa.com.

How can I become a sponsor?

We welcome sponsorship inquiries! Please select "Sponsorship" as your subject in the contact form, and our partnership team will reach out with available opportunities.

Where are the matches played?

All matches are played at the College Field. The venue is equipped with proper facilities for players and spectators.

How are player transfers handled?

Player transfers are managed through the Player Market system. Contact player agents directly via WhatsApp for transfer negotiations.

Can I volunteer for the tournament?

Yes! We welcome volunteers. Please contact the Sports Director or use the contact form to express your interest in volunteering opportunities.
