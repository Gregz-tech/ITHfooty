Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can my team register for the tournament?

Team registration is handled through level coaches. Contact your level coach or the Sports Coordinator for registration details and deadlines.

Where are the matches played?

All matches are played at the College Field, OSSCE, Ila-Orangun or Bisi Akande in some cases. The venue is easily accessible to all students.

Can students from other departments participate?

This is an intra-departmental tournament exclusively for ITH students. However, students from other departments are welcome to spectate and support.

How are match schedules determined?

Schedules are created considering academic calendars, venue availability, and fair rest periods between matches for all teams.

What happens in case of unforseen circumstances?

Matches may be postponed due to unforeseen circumstances. Updates will be communicated via WhatsApp groups and social media channels.
