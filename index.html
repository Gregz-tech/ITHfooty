About The Tournament

The ITH Football Tournament is an annual sporting event organized as part of the departmental-Week events by the department of Information Technology and Health Informatics (ITH), Federal University of Health Sciences, Ila-Orangun, It brings together students across all academic levels to compete, celebrate teamwork, and strengthen departmental unity through the spirit of football, and some other outdoor activitie.
