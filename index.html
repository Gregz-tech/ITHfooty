About The Tournament

The FUHSIITHISA Football Tournament is an annual inter-departmental competition that brings together students from all levels to showcase their football talents, foster unity, and promote sportsmanship. This year's edition promises to be the most exciting yet with enhanced competitions and amazing prizes.
